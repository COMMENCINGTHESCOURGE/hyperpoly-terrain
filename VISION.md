# VISION: The Continuity Engine

## Core Doctrine
**This is a continuity engine, not a game engine.** 

`hyperpoly-terrain` is not designed to just render polygons; it is designed to compute the unbroken, thermodynamic continuity of matter. The fundamental paradigm shift is treating terrain and world structures as mathematical tensors flowing through WebGPU (and eventually native backends), rather than static vertex buffers manipulated by a CPU.

## The Pangea Principle
We reject the illusion of "levels" and "loading screens." The universe is a continuous manifold. `hyperpoly-terrain` enables infinite, stigmergic obstacle routing, continuous geometric deformation, and thermodynamic decay, calculated entirely on the GPU.

## The 6-Channel Tensor Reference
Traditional game engines use simple XYZ coordinates and basic materials. We use a 6-channel tensor architecture:
1. **Geometry (XYZ)** - The spatial grid.
2. **Density** - Structural integrity and mass.
3. **Entropy** - Decay rate, heat simulation, and time-based erosion.
4. **Viscosity** - Fluid dynamic resistance.
5. **Conductivity** - Energy transfer (heat, electricity, magic).
6. **Vinculum Link** - The programmatic tag binding this tensor to high-level metadata schedulers.

By treating the world as a 6-channel tensor field, we move from "rendering a mesh" to "simulating a substrate."
